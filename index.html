Etsy Seller Tools

Automated Listing Backup with Versioning & Full Metadata Support

About

Etsy Seller Tools is a web application designed to help Etsy sellers protect their valuable shop data by providing automated backup solutions for their product listings.

Running an Etsy shop involves significant time and effort in creating detailed product listings with descriptions, photos, tags, and pricing information. This tool ensures that all of your hard work is safely backed up with complete version history, giving you peace of mind and the ability to track changes over time.

Key Features

Automatic Backups

Scheduled automatic backups of all your Etsy listings, ensuring your data is always protected without manual intervention.

Version History

Track changes to your listings over time with comprehensive version control, allowing you to see what changed and when.

Full Metadata Support

Complete backup of all listing data including titles, descriptions, photos, tags, pricing, inventory, and more.

Easy Restoration

Quickly restore previous versions of your listings if needed, recovering from accidental changes or deletions.

Purpose

This application integrates with the Etsy API to provide sellers with a reliable backup solution. By maintaining historical records of your listings, you can:

  • Protect against accidental deletions or unwanted changes
  • Analyze how your listings have evolved over time
  • Maintain records for business and tax purposes
  • Restore previous versions of listings with confidence
